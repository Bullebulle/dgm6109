Smart Kitchen

Small tips, big impact — little tricks that make everyday cooking easier!

Kitchen Basics

1.Always preheat your pan — ingredients cook more evenly.

2.Season gradually; you can always add more, but never take it out.

3.Let cooked food rest a minute to keep juices and flavor inside.

4.Taste as you cook — it's the simplest way to improve any dish.

5.Use low heat for sauces to avoid burning and bitterness.

Knife Skills

1.Hold the knife close to the blade for better control.

2.Keep your fingers curled inward — “the claw” protects your hand.

3.Let the knife do the work; don't push straight down.

4.Wipe the blade, don't flick it — it's safer and cleaner.

5.A sharp knife is safer than a dull one — sharpen regularly.
